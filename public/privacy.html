Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

What we collect

When you use HelixIQ, we collect only what is necessary to deliver your report:

  • Email address — provided at checkout so we can deliver your report PDF.
  • Raw DNA file — uploaded by you after payment, used solely to generate your report.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details.

How we handle your DNA data

Your raw DNA file is the most sensitive data you can share. We treat it accordingly:

  • Your file is processed in memory and permanently deleted the moment your report is generated and emailed.
  • We do not write your DNA data to disk, archive it, or back it up.
  • Once your report is sent, there is no copy of your raw file on our servers. It is gone.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data to pharmaceutical companies, insurers, researchers, or any third party.
  • We do not share your genetic information with anyone for any reason.
  • We do not create a user account or profile for you. There is no login, no dashboard, and no stored data after delivery.
  • We do not use your data for research, training, or any purpose beyond generating the report you purchased.

Payment processing

All payments are processed by Stripe, the same payment infrastructure used by Amazon, Google, and millions of other businesses. We never receive, see, or store your credit card number, expiration date, or CVV. Stripe's privacy policy governs payment data.

Email

We use your email address solely to deliver your report. We do not add you to a mailing list, send marketing emails, or share your email with third parties. After your report is delivered, we retain your email only in our payment records through Stripe.

Cookies and tracking

HelixIQ does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party scripts that monitor your behavior. The site loads fonts from Google Fonts — that is the only external request made.

Data retention

  • DNA files — deleted immediately after report generation. Zero retention.
  • Generated reports — emailed to you and then not stored on our servers.
  • Payment records — retained by Stripe per their data retention policies and applicable financial regulations.

Your rights

Because we do not retain your genetic data or create user profiles, there is nothing to request deletion of. If you have any questions about your data or want to confirm that nothing is stored, contact us at support@myhelixiq.com.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated clearly on our website.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, email support@myhelixiq.com.
